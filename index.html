4 — Failure Modes

What goes wrong when optimization succeeds?

Reward Hacking
Policy exploits RM quirks.
RM score ↑, actual quality ↓
Overoptimization
Too many steps against a proxy reward. Goodhart's law.
KL Collapse
Weak KL penalty → policy drifts far from base, loses coherence.
Mode Collapse
Converges to one "safe" style. Diversity gone.
Distribution Shift
RM trained on base policy outputs, but policy has moved.
Preference Collapse
Annotators disagree. Model learns the average → bland.

All of these are symptoms of optimizing too hard against an imperfect signal.
